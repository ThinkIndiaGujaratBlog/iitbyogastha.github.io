Date: 31st March 2018 (Saturday)

Venue: 3rd floor, Multi Purpose Hall, New Gymkhana Building, Opp. Hostel 11.


Register Rules

Rules for Yog Pratiyogita

  1. The participants must perform any four asanas from the list for their respective age group.
  2. The participant will be given three minutes to demonstrate the four asanas in front of the judges.
  3. Gold, silver and bronze medals for top three winners in male and female category in each group.
  4. The decision made by the judges will be considered final.
Group 1 (Age 14-24)
  1. Dhanurasana
  2. Sirshasana
  3. Mayurasana
  4. Bakasana
  5. Hanumanasana
  6. Baddha Padmasana
  7. Niralamba Paschimottanasana
  8. Sarvangasana
Group 2 (Age 25-35)
  1. Chakrasana
  2. Bakasana
  3. Mayurasana
  4. Sirshasana
  5. Garudasana
  6. Yoga Mudrasana
  7. Druta Utkatasana
  8. Dwikonasana
Group 3 (Age above 35)
  1. Paschimottanasana
  2. Vipareeta Karani Asana
  3. Supta Vajrasana
  4. Chakrasana
  5. Eka pada Pranamasana
  6. Lolasana
  7. Trikonasana
  8. Gomukhasana

Separate category for male and female participants in each group.
Prizes to be won in each category!!
