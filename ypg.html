Rules for Yog Pratiyogita

Rules for Yog Pratiyogita

  1. The participants must perform any four asanas from the list for their respective age group.
  2. The participant will be given three minutes to demonstrate the four asanas in front of the judges.
  3. Gold, silver and bronze medals for top three winners in male and female category in each group.
  4. The decision made by the judges will be considered final.
